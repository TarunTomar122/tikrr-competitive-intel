Benzinga Pro

Real-time market news for active traders — audio squawk and news edge

What They Do

Benzinga Pro is a real-time market news platform designed for active traders who need to react quickly to market-moving events. Its standout features include the "Why Is It Moving?" (WIIM) tool that explains sudden stock moves, audio squawk (live audio news broadcast), and real-time news alerts across 28 categories. It's positioned as a budget alternative to Bloomberg Terminal.

Core features: Real-time newsfeed (28 categories), WIIM (Why Is It Moving), audio squawk, calendar (earnings/events), signals, scanner, watchlist alerts.

Marketing Strategy

  • Content marketing: Benzinga.com is a major financial news site that drives traffic to Pro.
  • SEO: News articles rank for trending financial topics. The free news site acts as funnel.
  • YouTube: Active YouTube presence with market analysis and platform demos.
  • Affiliate: Partnerships with trading educators and review sites.
  • Events: Benzinga hosts financial events and conferences for brand awareness.

Reddit Complaints

Expensive for individual traders

The Essential plan at $117-177/mo is prohibitive for most individual traders. The Basic plan at $27-37/mo is limited in features.

Not a brokerage — need separate accounts

Users must maintain separate brokerage accounts since Benzinga Pro is a news/data platform, not a trading platform. This adds friction.
