Finviz

Financial Visualizations — Stock screener, heatmaps, and market maps

What They Do

Finviz (Financial Visualizations) is one of the oldest and most recognized stock screening platforms, launched in 1998. It's best known for its iconic market heatmaps and powerful stock screener that lets users filter stocks by 60+ technical and fundamental criteria. The platform serves swing traders, day traders, and long-term investors who need quick visual overviews of market conditions.

Core features: Stock screener (60+ filters), market heatmaps, charting with pattern recognition, backtesting (Elite), insider trading dashboards, futures/forex/crypto data, news aggregator.

Marketing Strategy

  • SEO: Dominates "stock screener", "stock heatmap", "market heatmap" keywords. Pages rank on page 1 of Google for dozens of high-volume financial terms.
  • Organic/Viral: Heatmaps are widely shared on Reddit, Twitter, and financial blogs. The visual nature makes it inherently shareable.
  • Content: Minimal content marketing. Relies on the tool itself as the content. No blog, no YouTube, no social media strategy.
  • Paid Ads: Limited. Appears in some financial comparison articles via affiliate partnerships.
  • Affiliate Program: Active affiliate program with bloggers and review sites driving sign-ups.
