StockAnalysis.com

"Yahoo Finance but better" — Clean, fast stock data for DIY investors

What They Do

StockAnalysis.com is a minimalist stock research platform founded in 2019 by Kris Gunnars. It's built a reputation as one of the cleanest, fastest platforms for researching stocks and ETFs. Covering 130,000+ global stocks and funds, it pulls data from S&P Global and Nasdaq Data Link. The design philosophy is "Bloomberg-lite" for retail investors — fast page loads, minimal clutter, comprehensive data.

Core features: Stock screener (290+ filters), financial statements (10-40 years), dividend history, insider activity, analyst ratings, IPO data, ETF analysis, data export.

Marketing Strategy

  • SEO-first: Individual stock pages rank well for "[ticker] stock" queries. 130K+ pages create massive organic traffic.
  • Product-led growth: The product is the marketing. Fast, clean UX drives word-of-mouth.
  • Review sites: 4.7-4.9 stars on Trustpilot (194 reviews) drives trust.
  • Minimal marketing spend: Small team, no paid ads. Relies on organic growth and reviews.
  • Affiliate partnerships: Finance bloggers and review sites drive sign-ups.
