Koyfin

Bloomberg-lite for retail investors — global market analytics platform

What They Do

Koyfin is a global market analytics platform that aims to provide Bloomberg Terminal-level data to retail investors at a fraction of the cost. It covers stocks, ETFs, bonds, forex, commodities, and macroeconomic data with advanced charting and screening capabilities. The platform is praised for its intuitive interface and breadth of data coverage.

Core features: Multi-asset charting, stock screener, macro dashboards, ETF analysis, bond data, forex/commodities, custom dashboards, portfolio analytics.
