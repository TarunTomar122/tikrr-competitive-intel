TipRanks

AI-powered analyst accountability — tracking 96,000+ financial experts

What They Do

TipRanks, founded in 2012, uses AI to track and rank the performance of 96,000+ financial experts including Wall Street analysts, bloggers, insiders, and hedge fund managers. The platform's "Financial Accountability Engine" scans financial websites, SEC filings, and analyst ratings to measure who's actually worth listening to. Acquired by Prytek for $200M in 2024.

Core features: Analyst rankings, Smart Score (1-10 composite rating), stock analysis, insider trading tracking, hedge fund tracking, earnings forecasts, news sentiment, portfolio monitoring.
