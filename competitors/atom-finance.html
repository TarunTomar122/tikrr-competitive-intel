Atom Finance

Institutional data for retail investors — Bloomberg Terminal alternative (acquired by Togal.AI)

What They Do

Atom Finance bridges the gap between retail and institutional investors by providing tools and research typically unavailable to individual traders. Often called "Bloomberg Terminal for retail investors," it offers Sandbox (financial modeling), portfolio tracking, X-Ray (deep company analysis), stock screening, and community chat. In 2024, Atom Finance was acquired by Togal.AI.

Core features: Sandbox financial modeling, portfolio tracker, X-Ray company analysis, stock screener, Finance Chat (community), Hubs (curated research), Discord bot, mobile app.

TraderHQ review

Small user base and community

Compared to TradingView (100M+ users) or Seeking Alpha (20M+), Atom Finance has a much smaller community, meaning less user-generated content and fewer perspectives.

Various reviews

Desktop-focused experience

While there's a mobile app, the full Atom experience (especially Sandbox modeling) is designed for desktop. Mobile users get a limited experience.
