Finviz has been the go-to stock screener for retail investors since 2007. tikrr is a newer market intelligence platform built around AI briefs and watchlist radar. They solve different problems. Here's how to pick the right one.

Overview

Finviz

What it is: Stock screener with heatmaps, news aggregation, and market visualization.

Best for: Finding stocks that match specific criteria (P/E ratio, sector, volume, technical patterns).

Core strength: The screener. 8,000+ stocks filtered by 60+ criteria in real-time (Elite).

Pricing: Free (delayed) / $39.50/mo Elite

tikrr

What it is: Market intelligence platform with AI briefs, watchlist radar, and sector spotlights.

Best for: Understanding what's happening in the market right now and what it means for your portfolio.

Core strength: AI-powered daily briefs that curate market intelligence so you don't have to.

Pricing: Free tier / $9/mo Pro

Who Each Tool Is For

Choose Finviz If You...

  • Need a stock screener with advanced filters
  • Trade based on technical patterns and chart setups
  • Want Finviz's iconic heatmap visualization
  • Do deep fundamental research on individual stocks
  • Prefer to do your own analysis (not AI-curated)

Choose tikrr If You...

  • Want daily market intelligence without doing the work
  • Have a watchlist and want AI to monitor it for you
  • Prefer curated briefs over raw data feeds
  • Don't have time to check 5 different sites every morning
  • Want market intelligence at a lower price point

The Verdict

They Solve Different Problems

Finviz is a screener. It answers "show me stocks that match my criteria." tikrr is an intelligence platform. It answers "what's happening in the market and what does it mean for me?"

If you're an active trader who scans for setups, Finviz is essential. If you're an investor who wants to understand the market in 5 minutes every morning, tikrr is the better fit. Many users will benefit from both — Finviz for screening, tikrr for intelligence.

At $9/mo vs $39.50/mo, tikrr is also the clear value play. You get AI-powered intelligence for less than a quarter of Finviz's price.
