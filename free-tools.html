🔍 The Free Tool Pattern

What Works for Lead Gen

  • No-signup tools — Finviz screener, Yahoo quotes get massive traffic without asking for email
  • Generous free tier — TradingView hooks users with real value, then limits advanced features
  • SEO-driven tools — Yahoo Finance "[ticker] stock price" pages capture millions of searches
  • Shareable outputs — TradingView charts and TipRanks analyst pages get shared on social media
  • Content metering — Seeking Alpha's 5 free articles/month creates FOMO

What Doesn't Work

  • Too-gated — Requiring signup for basic data kills conversion
  • Useless free tier — If free version can't demonstrate value, users leave
  • Ads everywhere — Finviz's free tier is so ad-heavy it hurts trust
  • Bait and switch — Seeking Alpha's aggressive billing creates backlash
  • No upgrade path — Free tools that don't naturally lead to paid features

🚀 Free Tools tikrr Should Build

These free tools would drive organic traffic, build trust, and convert users to Pro.

1. Free Daily Market Brief (Email) TOP PRIORITY

What: A free daily email with the market overview, top movers, and one AI insight. No signup for web version, email signup for delivery.

Why: This is tikrr's core product. Give away a lite version to demonstrate value. The full brief (watchlist radar, sector spotlights, alerts) stays behind Pro.

SEO play: Publish the brief as a daily blog post. Target "daily market summary [date]" and "market brief today" keywords. Each post is fresh, indexed content.

Conversion: Free brief shows market overview. Pro brief includes YOUR watchlist, YOUR sectors, YOUR alerts. The personalization is the upgrade.

2. Market Intelligence Scorecard (Free Widget) HIGH

What: A free embeddable widget showing the 4-pillar scorecard for any ticker. Users type a ticker, get a quick health score.

Why: Scorecards are visual, shareable, and demonstrate AI value instantly. Like TipRanks' Smart Score but free and embeddable.

SEO play: Create "/score/AAPL" pages for top 500 tickers. Each page targets "[ticker] score" or "[ticker] health check" keywords.

Conversion: Free scorecard for 3 tickers. Pro unlocks unlimited tickers + historical tracking + alerts on score changes.

3. Sector Heatmap (Free, No Signup) HIGH

What: A visual heatmap of market sectors showing daily performance, like Finviz's heatmap but for sectors instead of individual stocks.

Why: Heatmaps are the most shareable financial visualization. Finviz proves this with their stock heatmap. Sector-level is less competitive.

SEO play: Target "market sector heatmap," "sector performance today," "S&P 500 sectors" keywords. Daily updates = fresh content.

Conversion: Free heatmap shows today's data. Pro unlocks historical trends, alerts when sectors break patterns, sector comparison tools.

4. AI Watchlist (Free, 5 Tickers) MEDIUM

What: Free watchlist for up to 5 tickers with AI scoring on fundamentals and basic alerts.

Why: 5 tickers is enough to demonstrate value. Users with larger portfolios need Pro.

SEO play: "/watchlist" landing page targeting "AI stock watchlist" and "smart watchlist stock" keywords.

Conversion: Free = 5 tickers, daily email summary. Pro = unlimited tickers, real-time alerts, breakouts/RSI/earnings alerts.

5. Market Calendar (Free) MEDIUM

What: Free earnings calendar, economic events calendar, IPO calendar with AI context on why each event matters.

Why: Calendars drive recurring visits. Users check them daily. The AI context angle is unique (competitors just list events).

SEO play: Target "earnings calendar this week," "economic calendar today," "upcoming IPOs" keywords.

Conversion: Free = calendar view. Pro = alerts on events affecting YOUR watchlist + AI brief mentions upcoming events.

6. Market News Digest (Free, No Signup) LOW

What: A curated daily news page with AI-generated summaries of top market stories. 5-10 stories, each with a 2-sentence AI summary.

Why: Competitors show raw news feeds. AI summaries are the differentiation. This drives SEO traffic from "market news today" queries.

SEO play: Daily news digest pages target "stock market news today" and "market recap [date]" keywords.

Conversion: Free = general market news. Pro = news filtered to YOUR watchlist + alerts when news affects your positions.

💡 The Key Insight

Every competitor's free tier is either too restricted (useless without paying) or too generic (same data as everyone else). tikrr's free tools should be genuinely useful but personally limited.

The pattern: give away the market-level intelligence for free (overview, sectors, news). Charge for the personal intelligence (your watchlist, your alerts, your brief). The free tools demonstrate AI value; the paid tools deliver AI value to you specifically.
